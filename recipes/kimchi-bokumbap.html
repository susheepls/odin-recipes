Mother's Kimchi Fried Rice Recipe

Kimchi Bokumbap(kimchi fried rice) is one of my favorite meals of all time. The tasty rice filled with different vegetables and meats all come together with a nice egg on top. The name is also really fun to say outloud.

With my recipe, I will tell you how my mother used to make this delicious meal. Whenever she made this meal, I would start drooling from just the scent, and I hope you will too.

Ingredients

  • Yummy Kimchi
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Olive Oil
  • Garlice powder
  • Soy Sauce
  • Meats (optional)
  • Pan and stove
  • Bowl

Steps to create my childhood dish

  1. Prep frying pan with olive oil by moving it around
  2. Put eggs on and then cover until well done. Put them aside
  3. Put kimchi into a bowl
  4. Cut kimchi wtih scissors in the bowl until they are very small
  5. Prepare another pan and butter
  6. Put in a half stick or more of butter into the pan proceeded by the kimchi
  7. Stir for a long time
  8. Put in garlic powder
  9. Stir Longer
  10. Put in rice and soy sauce
  11. Stir for an even longer time
  12. Afterwards, get a plate and dump ur rice on it
  13. Cover your meal with the eggs and VIOLA! Enjoy the meal

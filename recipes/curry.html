My go-to Curry concotion

One of my favorite meals in Japan is curry over rice. It doesn't have to have katsu on top, and it can be a healthy meal depending on its content. Ignore the picture; its just my favorite place to eat curry if im not cooking.

In this recipe, we will make sure that the curry contains a healthy amount of vegetables and meats. Doing so, you can create a delcious,well balanced meal that you will love eating every night.

Ingredients

  • Brocolli
  • Potato
  • Carrot
  • Onions
  • Chicken meat: Boneless leg
  • Half stick of Butter
  • Curry sauce mix
  • a Big bowl
  • a Nice pot
  • Wooden stir stick

Steps to create this Beauty

  1. Peel potatos
  2. Peel carrots
  3. Peel Onions
  4. Wash all veggies with veggie wash: including the brocolli. (use Vinegar if there is no veggie wash)
  5. Cut potatos (vertically half and vertically half again..,then cut horizontally)
  6. Cut carrots
  7. Cut onions (in half,then vertically half, then at an angle)
  8. Cut chicken into any sizes you want
  9. Put all into a big bowl
  10. Put half stick of butter into cooking pot..wait a bit then stir it around
  11. Put the rest of the ingredients inside
  12. Stir at intervals
  13. Once they all look decently cooked,put water into the pot
  14. After boiling, put curry sauce mix
  15. Stir more
  16. Then lower heat and let it simmer for a while until veggies are soft
  17. VIOLA!!! Enjoy your curry with rice or whatever

My go-to Curry concotion

One of my favorite meals in Japan is curry over rice. It doesn't have to have katsu on top, and it can be a healthy meal depending on its content. Ignore the picture; its just my favorite place to eat curry if im not cooking.

In this recipe, we will make sure that the curry contains a healthy amount of vegetables and meats. Doing so, you can create a delcious,well balanced meal that you will love eating every night.

Ingredients

  • Brocolli
  • Potato
  • Carrot
  • Chicken meat: Boneless leg
  • Half stick of Butter
  • Curry sauce mix
  • a Nice pot
  • Wooden stir stick

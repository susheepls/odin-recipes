My Favorite Potato Side Dish

This is a recipe for my favroite side dish that my mother used to make. It is very simple but delicious, and can compliment any other meal, especially meats.

I hope that you will be able to realize that cooking has the ability to make simple veggies into a powerhouse of flavor and satisfaction.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Baby Potatos
  • Olive oil
  • Parsley
  • Onions
  • Salt/Pepper
  • Frying pan

Steps for the godly sidedish

  1. Wash potatos
  2. Cut them into thin pieces
  3. Warm frying pan then add olive oil
  4. Wait for pan to become hot
  5. Place potatos on pan onto they become yellow
  6. Sprinkle salt and pepper on top
  7. Add butter (one spoon scoop)
  8. After the butter melts, add parsley and onion
  9. Move around the pan
  10. DONE
